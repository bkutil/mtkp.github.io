Contact

First, check that the book is available in the table below.

Then shoot me an email to balazs@kutilovi.cz with the name of the book, ideally a day in advance.

Books can be picked up and returned on Mondays. 3-5 p.m, Smetanova 78⁄1, Písek. My office doesn’t have a doorbell, so call me at +420 774 788 228 when you arrive and I’ll navigate you around.

Terms

You can borrow the book for a month. If you are new to the library, there’s a 500 CZK deposit. It’ll be returned to you after you return the book. In case the book is lost, or significantly damaged, the deposit will be used to order a new copy.
